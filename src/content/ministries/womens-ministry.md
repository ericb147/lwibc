## Our Vision

To create a community where women of all ages and life stages can grow in their relationship with Christ, support one another, and discover their God-given purpose, ultimately becoming a Titus 2 woman.

## Core Focus Areas

### Spiritual Growth
- Bible study and prayer
- Discipleship and mentorship
- Spiritual gifts discovery
- Personal relationship with Christ

### Community and Fellowship
- Building authentic friendships
- Supporting one another through life's challenges
- Celebrating milestones and achievements
- Developing an inclusive and welcoming environment

### Service and Outreach
- Using gifts to serve others
- Community involvement
- Missions support
- Acts of kindness and compassion

## Regular Programs

### Ladies' Fellowship
**When**: Fourth Friday of each month, 7:00 PM
**What**:
- Bible study and discussion
- Prayer time
- Fellowship and refreshments
